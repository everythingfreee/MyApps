Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Video Downloader ("the App") handles information when you use it. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

Summary

  • We do not collect or sell personal data.
  • All processing is performed on-device; your downloads and history remain on your device unless you choose to share them.
  • We request only the permissions required for core functionality (see Permissions We Use).

If your build includes analytics, ads, crash reporting, or external SDKs, edit the sections below accordingly. Remove any parts that do not apply.

Information We Collect

The App itself does not collect, transmit, or store personal information on our servers. Downloaded files, preferences, and history are stored locally on your device.

Third‑Party Services

By default, the App does not integrate external analytics or advertising SDKs.

Permissions We Use

  • Internet – to connect to sources and download files.
  • Read/Write Storage (or Photos and Videos access on newer Android) – to save downloaded files to your device.
  • Foreground service / Notifications – to show download progress and completion status.

You can revoke permissions at any time via your device settings, but some features may stop working.

User Responsibilities & Lawful Use

The App is intended for personal use. You are responsible for ensuring that your use complies with applicable laws and the terms of any platform or website. Do not download or distribute content without permission from the rights holder.

Data Security

We apply best‑practice security measures within the App. Because we do not operate a backend for this App, we do not store your data on external servers. Protect your device with a passcode and keep your operating system up to date.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe a child has provided personal information within the App, please contact us so we can help.

Your Choices & Rights

Because the App does not collect personal data on our servers, requests to access, correct, or delete data typically involve managing content stored locally on your device. If you have questions, contact us using the details below.

Changes to This Policy
